Student personal information panel

Student examination panel

This option can provide the information about the examination those have conducted or are conducting.

Student assignment panel

This option is used to represent the assignment details which are submitted.

Student registration panel

This option is used to represent the registration details.

Student project panel

This option is used to represent the details of the projects made by the students.

This option is used to represent the details of the study material opted by the students.

Concerns/queries panel

This is the panel from where the concerns can be written and can be passed to the Administrator.

Conversation panel

This option shows the conversation details of the staff members.

Email templates panel

This is the option to represents the desired email templates given by the management.
